## Description

This gear is part of a double complex worm gear system. A worm is attached to a worm wheel, and an extra worm is mounted on top of it to create a multi-stage reduction mechanism.

## Design Details

- Mechanism type: double complex worm gear system
- Number of worms driving the gear: one worm drives one worm wheel in each stage
- Number of stages: four worm gear stages
- Target reduction ratio: 100:1
- Total reduction ratio: 100:1
- Module / pitch / tooth profile: worm wheel with 100 teeth, module 1 mm, standard involute tooth profile
- Shaft diameter / mounting: press-fit onto a 10 mm shaft or PVC pipe

## Critical Dimensions

- Worm wheel teeth: 100
- Module: 1 mm
- Pitch diameter: 100 mm
- Outer diameter: 102 mm
- Face width: 10 mm
- Bore diameter: 10 mm

## Materials & Print Settings

- Preferred material: PLA

## Assembly Notes


- Prototype support: 12 mm steel pipe 240 mm length
- Shims / spacers: small spacers or shims may be added for alignment and smooth meshing.

## Testing & Validation

- All gear stages should mesh correctly.
- The prototype should demonstrate the intended reduction ratio.
